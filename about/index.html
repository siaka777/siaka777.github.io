The story so far

More than ten years in software engineering has taken me across the full stack — from architecting distributed systems to shipping frontend experiences — but what has stayed constant is a deliberate pull toward the edges of my knowledge. I don't just want to be good at what I already know. I want to be uncomfortable in the best possible way.

Right now that discomfort looks like diving deep into AI and machine learning, exploring web security and cybersecurity principles, and pushing the boundaries of what I can build on the web. Each domain feeds the others in ways I never expect.

How I learn

There's no single method that works for everything. Some concepts click through building — a quick prototype that breaks in interesting ways. Others need slow reading, a paper printed out with margin notes. Others need a structured course to give them shape. I use all of it, switching modes based on the material.
