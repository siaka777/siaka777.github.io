💡 Key Exam Tips
CRISC emphasizes the business perspective — always link IT risk to enterprise objectives
Risk appetite vs tolerance: appetite is strategic, tolerance is the acceptable variance
Remember: inherent risk → control → residual risk is the core framework
The risk owner is accountable; the risk practitioner advises and supports
KRIs are leading indicators; KPIs are lagging — know when each is appropriate
